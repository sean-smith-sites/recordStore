New to a Very Old Media?

Bomb Fire is here to help. Here you will find the general knowledge questions you were too afraid to ask your local jaded record store clerk. This is a spot to get your lingo down, know what you're buying and how to a take care of the waxy treasure once you get it home. Get tips for hunting in the wilds of thrift stores and flea markets as well. What we want for you is to embrace the analog life and look past the convenience of streaming and find the warm happiness vinyl can lay on you.
